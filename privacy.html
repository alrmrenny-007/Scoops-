Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy explains what information Scoops Chops & Drinks × Mr. Jollof ("Scoops," "we," "us") collects when you use our website to browse the menu or place an order, and what we do with it. We're a local restaurant in Makurdi, Benue State — this policy is written plainly, not as legal boilerplate, so you actually know what's going on.

Information we collect

When you place an order, whether as a guest or with an account, we collect:

  • Your name and phone number — so we can reach you about your order.
  • Your delivery address — if you choose delivery rather than pickup.
  • Your order details — items, quantities, and any notes you add.
  • Your email address — only if you create an account.

We don't collect payment card details on this site — orders are currently settled on delivery or pickup.

How we use your information

  • To prepare and deliver your order.
  • To contact you if there's a question or issue with your order, including via phone call or WhatsApp.
  • If you create an account, to save your details so checkout is faster next time and so you can see your order history.

We do not sell your information to anyone, and we do not use it for advertising.

Where your information is stored

Your data is stored using Supabase, a third-party database provider, with access controls limiting it to authorized staff. If you create an account, your password is encrypted and never visible to us in plain text. If you use the "Send order to WhatsApp" button, the order summary is sent through WhatsApp (operated by Meta) to our business number — that message is subject to WhatsApp's own privacy practices once sent.

Local storage ("cookies")

This site uses your browser's local storage — technically not the same as a traditional cookie, but serving a similar purpose — to remember your cart contents, dark/light theme preference, and login session. This data stays on your device and is used only to make the site work properly. You can clear it anytime by clearing your browser's site data.

How long we keep your information

Order records are kept for our business records and to resolve any disputes. If you'd like your account and associated data deleted, contact us using the details below and we'll action it.

Your rights

You can ask us at any time to:

  • Tell you what information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your account and associated data.

Children

This site is intended for general audiences placing food orders and isn't directed at children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page.
